# Audio Files for English Placement Test

This directory contains audio files for the listening comprehension section of the English placement test.

## Audio Files

- `l-a1-01.mp3` - Basic personal information (A1 level)
  - Transcript: "Hi, my name is Tom. I am from London. I am 22 years old."
  - Duration: ~10 seconds
  
- `l-b1-01.mp3` - Workplace conversation (B1 level)
  - Transcript: "In today's meeting, the team agreed to push the product launch back by two weeks. The main reason was that the QA team needed more time to fix critical bugs before release."
  - Duration: ~15 seconds
  
- `l-b2-01.mp3` - News/opinion piece (B2 level)
  - Transcript: "While the new tax reform is intended to reduce income inequality, critics argue that without addressing wealth concentration at the top, the reform will have a limited impact on lower-income households in the long run."
  - Duration: ~20 seconds

- `l-c1-01.mp3` - Workplace trends (C1 level)
  - Transcript: "Although remote work has increased productivity for many employees, it has also blurred the boundaries between professional and personal life. As a result, companies are now exploring hybrid models that aim to balance flexibility with the need for in-person collaboration."
  - Duration: ~20 seconds

- `l-c2-01.mp3` - Policy and technology (C2 level)
  - Transcript: "In light of the accelerating pace of technological innovation, it is imperative that policymakers adopt a proactive rather than reactive approach. Failure to anticipate the societal implications of emerging technologies could exacerbate existing inequalities and create challenges that are far more difficult to address retrospectively."
  - Duration: ~25 seconds

## Audio Specifications

- Format: MP3
- Duration: 10-25 seconds each
- Quality: Clear speech, minimal background noise
- Level: Progressive difficulty from A1 to C2

## Creating Audio Files

You can:
1. Use text-to-speech tools (Google TTS, Amazon Polly, ElevenLabs)
2. Record with native speakers
3. Use free educational audio resources
